HEALTH AND SAFETY NOTICE

To ensure both your safety and wellbeing and that of our team members, we ask that if you feel unwell or have flu like symptoms prior to your appointment, that you call us to reschedule to a later date.

Rest assured that Connect Hearing is fully committed to maintaining rigorous standards and hygiene practices in line with all Government guidelines to ensure the safety and wellbeing of our customers and clinic team members.

Please maintain Social Distancing requirements when in clinic, this may mean that only one person is able to be in the waiting room at a time in some locations.
